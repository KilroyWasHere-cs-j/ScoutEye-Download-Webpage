ScoutEye

ScoutEye is a scouting app for FRC teams. It is designed to be lightweight and easy to use. It is capable of beginning run both desktop and mobile devices.

Desktop

Enjoy the comfort and familiarity of a desktop experience with ScoutEye. Support for both Windows and Linux.

Android

For your mobile needs ScoutEye is capable of running on Android devices. iOS support is coming soon.

Customization

ScoutEye is designed to be customizable! You can change the color scheme, add custom fields, and more!
